# Monitoring & Audit Design

## Responsibilities
- Collect system and strategy metrics
- Maintain immutable audit logs
- Provide incident timelines

## Inputs
- Metrics/events from all components

## Outputs
- Alerts and dashboards
- Audit records

## Failure Modes
- Missing events
- Alert fatigue
- Audit gaps

## Metrics
- Alert precision
- Audit completeness
- MTTR
